Legacy of Classic Arcade Games

A focused look at the games themselves — the titles, mechanics and moments that became the touchstones of arcade history.

Iconic Games That Defined an Era

Pac-Man (1980)

A cultural and design milestone: simple rules, deep patterns, and characters that became globally recognized.

Space Invaders (1978)

Helped create the shoot 'em up template and demonstrated how score-driven gameplay could hook players for hours.

Street Fighter II (1991)

Turned arcades into competitive hubs by formalizing fighting mechanics, combos and character matchups.

Donkey Kong (1981)

Introduced structured levels and a narrative hook, paving the way for character-driven game design.

Quick Timeline

1972: Pong launches the commercial arcade market.
1978–1983: Golden age titles set gameplay templates and arcade culture.
1990s: Competitive and genre-expanding arcade releases.
2000s–today: Preservation, re-releases and continued inspiration for designers.
