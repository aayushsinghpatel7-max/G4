Online Revolution

The true revolution of the last few decades wasn't just graphics; it was connection. The advent of broadband internet transformed gaming from a solitary hobby into a mass social experience. Persistent worlds, global competitive arenas, and entire digital economies were born, forever changing how we play, compete, and interact.

Gaming, the digital revolution

Living in Another World: World of Warcraft

"Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games" (MMORPGs) created persistent digital worlds where thousands of players coexisted. If Ultima Online (1997) wrote the rules, Blizzard's World of Warcraft (WoW) perfected the formula, making it accessible and engaging for millions. WoW wasn't just a game; it was a "second place" and a social phenomenon for an entire generation.
