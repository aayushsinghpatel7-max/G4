POWER ON

From buzzing arcades to handheld adventures and living room revolutions, video games evolved from flickering pixels into entire digital universes. This timeline explores the eras where innovation, obsession, and creativity merged into one industry that reshaped entertainment, culture, and technology.

Arcades: The First Battleground

Arcades were more than places to play — they were coliseums of glowing pixels. One coin meant one chance. Your audience was real, loud, and unforgiving. High scores were reputation. Cabinets were shrines.

The Shift to Home

Consoles brought the arcade home. No quarters. No crowds. Just cartridges, glowing TVs, and the quiet competition of siblings on the couch.

Pop Culture Power-Up

Arcades bled into movies, music, and fashion. They turned joystick legends into cultural icons and pixels into personality.
