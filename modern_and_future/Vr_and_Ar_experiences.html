VR & AR Experiences

Virtual Reality (VR) places you inside a simulated world. Augmented Reality (AR) layers digital content on top of the real world. Together they enable training, play, collaboration, and exploration in new ways.

Gaming & Entertainment

VR delivers presence and scale that flat screens can’t match—think rhythm slashing in Beat Saber, climbing in The Climb, or cockpit views in Gran Turismo 7 VR. AR keeps you in your space while adding characters, guides, or puzzles you can walk around.

Education & Training

VR simulations reduce risk and cost for aviation, medicine, and industrial safety. AR manuals overlay animated steps directly onto machines, shrinking the gap from novice to expert.

Tip: Pair VR with haptics or real tools (e.g., a prop drill) to increase skill transfer to the real world.

Design & Collaboration

Architects and product teams co‑create 1:1 scale models in VR. In AR, teams can place life‑size mockups in a room, move around them, and discuss changes naturally.

Healthcare & Well‑being

Exposure therapy, pain distraction, stroke rehab, and mindfulness environments use controlled VR scenarios that adapt to patient progress. AR helps visualize anatomy during procedures.

Tourism, Culture, and Real‑Estate

Walk ancient cities reconstructed in VR or preview an apartment in AR at true scale. Museums add AR labels and animated restorations to physical exhibits.
